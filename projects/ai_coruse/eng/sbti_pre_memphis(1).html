Introduction Slide 3 / 10

What is SBTI and why is it so popular?

lmzA

If you open social apps now, you can easily see many college students sharing SBTI test results. It has become a hot campus topic.

  • Fast and phone-friendly
  • Easy to discuss with friends
  • Feels trendy and fun
lmzB

SBTI means Silly Big Personality Test. It is an entertaining online quiz, not a strict scientific assessment.

  • Questions are humorous and daily-life based
  • Usually takes around 1-2 minutes
  • Good for fun, not for formal diagnosis

Reminder: SBTI is useful for entertainment and reflection, not as a scientific diagnosis.

Three Classic Types Slide 4 / 10

Malou, SEXY, and Mom

Malou

Lazy and casual. Avoids anxiety and keeps a laid-back rhythm in study and life.

SEXY

Confident and attractive. Pays attention to self-image and leaves a strong impression.

Mom

Warm and caring. Reminds friends to take care and is often the most thoughtful person in a group.

These three labels are common examples students often discuss online.

Advantages Slide 5 / 10

Why students enjoy SBTI

1) Convenient

  • No preparation needed
  • Can be done in short free time
  • Fits busy college schedules

2) Social Icebreaker

  • Easy way to start conversations
  • Helps find common topics quickly
  • Makes new friendships easier

3) Stress Relief

  • Funny labels create laughter
  • Light content lowers pressure
  • Helps release bad mood briefly
lmzA

These are the three major strengths: convenience, social connection, and stress relief.

Disadvantages Slide 6 / 10

What we should not ignore

lmzB
  • No strict scientific basis: results are random and cannot fully represent real personality.
  • Label limitation risk: over-believing one label can reduce growth motivation.
  • Unfair judging: using funny labels to define others is one-sided and inaccurate.

Over-identifying with labels may limit effort, confidence, and long-term growth.

Conclusion Slide 9 / 10

Enjoy the fun, keep clear judgment

lmzA

In a word, SBTI is an interesting entertainment tool. It can bring happiness and help us make friends more easily, but it has no scientific value.

lmzB

We can enjoy SBTI, but we should never take the results too seriously. As college students, we should know ourselves through real life and reflection.

Main takeaway

  • Use SBTI for fun conversation and stress release.
  • Avoid turning temporary labels into fixed identity.
  • Build self-knowledge through action, feedback, and reflection.
